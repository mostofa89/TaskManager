FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Task Manager free to use?

Yes! Task Manager offers a free plan with up to 50 tasks and basic features. Premium plans start at just $9/month for unlimited features.

Can I share tasks with my team?

Absolutely! Pro and Enterprise plans include team collaboration features. You can share tasks, assign work, and track progress together in real-time.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take security very seriously. Your data is encrypted, backed up daily, and stored on secure servers. We comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations.

Can I import my existing tasks?

Yes! We support importing from most popular task management platforms. You can also manually add tasks or use our CSV import feature.

What's the difference between the plans?

The Free plan is perfect for individuals with basic task management. Pro adds unlimited tasks and team collaboration. Enterprise includes dedicated support and API access.
